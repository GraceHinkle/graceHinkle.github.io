My Work


The Biomedical Research Institutes Websites

I work as a student Clerical Assistant for the IDeA Programs Office (IPO) within the Biomedical Research Institute (BRI) of BSU. Because of how big and different the branches of the BRI are, there are 3 main websites to organize and manage the different branches and their staff digitally. My work includes having to update and manage the main websites within wordpress and map any new additions. Recently the department rebranded so I was given the task of mapping and adjusting everything accordingly from titles to urls and making sure it all complied to BSU's website regulations.


Webpages


Biomedical Research Institute Webpage (BRI)

IDeA Programs Office Webpage (IPO)
