Art

I've been doing all kinds of art since I was a little girl. I have experience doing a ton of different traditional art, from oil painting to guache to colored pencils. However, for work, school and passion I've been focusing on a variety of digital art. Recently I've begun selling my art at conventions and I have always wanted to create comics or animations in some capacity and I am slowly learning the skills needed to do just that.

If I get chosen, I will only be selling prints, stickers, ect of my *digital art*. None of my traditional art, animations or 3D models.
