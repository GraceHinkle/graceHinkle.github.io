Me and My Art


I've been doing all kinds of art since I was a little girl. I have experience doing a ton of different traditional art, from oil painting to guache to colored pencils. However, for work, school and passion I've been focusing my college years on learning and improving my digital art.



Animation

Here are some little animations I have made over the past years. These were animations I challenged myself to complete less than a week. I also used them to excersize keeping things consistent even in different styles. All of these are frame by Frame and made in Photoshop.
