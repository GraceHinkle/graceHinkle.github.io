About Me

My name is Grace Hinkle and I mostly grew up in and currently live in Boise, Idaho. I am a Junior at Boise State University in the Gaming Interactive Media Mobile (GIMM) major. I work at the Boise State Biomedical Research Institute as a Clerical Assistant and website designer. I also worked within my major as a TA for the fall semester GIMM 100 classes and taught art classes on anatomy.

I have a lot of experience working with both physical and digital art, and am currently learning more about website design and videogame creation. I have traveled with my family a lot and even lived in Singapore for a year. I love a variety of different artforms, from traditional art to theater to music, and used to participate in a lot of them. Currently I am looking to get my Bachelors from BSU, make connections and hopefully get a job that will allow me to use some of my artistic skills.
